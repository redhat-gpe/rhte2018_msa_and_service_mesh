Microservices and Service Meshes

This multi-product lab has you secure and manage the services of a microservice architected application using a service mesh. Specifically, you will deploy and monitor your services in the mesh and visualize end-to-end traces of requests as they flow through each service. In addition, you will apply custom routing and test the resiliency between those services in the mesh.

The lab is deployed on Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform and includes the following components:
  • Red Hat OpenShift Application Runtimes
  • community Istio
